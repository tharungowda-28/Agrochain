Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AgroChain and how we operate.

General Questions

What is AgroChain?

AgroChain is a blockchain-based platform that connects farmers and consumers directly, providing transparency, fair pricing, and eliminating the need for middlemen. It aims to revolutionize the agricultural supply chain.

How does AgroChain work?

AgroChain uses blockchain to maintain a secure ledger of transactions. Farmers can list their products, and buyers can place orders, all while ensuring transparency and fair pricing for both parties. Payments are made securely through the platform.

Services

How do I upload my products?

Farmers can upload their products through the AgroChain dashboard. After signing up, simply click on the 'Upload Product' button, fill in the product details, and submit them for listing.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse available products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can securely make your payment and track your delivery once the order is confirmed.

Technical Support

What should I do if I'm facing technical issues with the website?

If you're experiencing technical issues with the website, please try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, you can contact our technical support team by email at support@agrochain.com for further assistance.

Who do I contact for technical issues?

For technical assistance, email support@agrochain.com, and our team will get back to you within 24 hours.

Billing & Payments

How do I make a payment?

Your payment will be processed securely through our platform upon successful delivery confirmation and it is done directly from buyers's account to farmers' account.

How are payments transferred to farmers?

Once the buyer confirms the delivery of the product, payments are transferred directly to the farmer's account through the platform's secure payment gateway.
